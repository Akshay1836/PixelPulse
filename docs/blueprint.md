# **App Name**: Aether Creative Studio

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Service Showcase: Present custom creative services through detailed pages, video showcases, masonry galleries, and project details, reflecting a luxury aesthetic.
- AI-Assisted Project Briefing Tool: A generative AI tool that guides potential service clients through a series of questions to formulate a comprehensive project brief, ensuring all requirements are captured efficiently.
- Digital Products E-commerce: Browse and filter instant-download digital assets (Lightroom Presets, LUTs, Canva Templates) on a dedicated shop page.
- Interactive Product Previews: Product detail pages featuring interactive before/after sliders for visual assets and multiple mockup screens for templates, allowing users to visualize the product's impact.
- Secure & Instant Checkout: A seamless one-page checkout integrated with Stripe or Razorpay, providing immediate on-screen and email delivery of download links for purchased digital products.
- Streamlined Service Inquiry: A smart inquiry form with conditional fields that directs client questions based on selected services, featuring direct WhatsApp integration and automated email notifications.
- Admin Sales & Lead Management: An administrative dashboard for managing digital products, tracking sales orders, overseeing service lead submissions, and viewing essential analytics. This uses Supabase (PostgreSQL) for data management.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color scheme: Dark theme based on a true black/charcoal aesthetic to evoke a cinematic, luxury feel. Primary background is absolute black (#000000), with '#0A0A0A' used for sectional differentiation.
- Primary interactive color: A rich, warm gold (#DA901A) provides high contrast against the dark background, used for key interactive elements and calls to action, drawing attention and suggesting quality.
- Accent color: A deep red-orange (#BF3A26) is used sparingly for highlights and important indicators, providing an energetic yet sophisticated pop against the primary and background hues.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif), chosen for its elegant, high-contrast, and fashionable aesthetic, reinforcing the studio's high-end brand.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif), providing excellent readability and a modern feel that complements the classic elegance of the headline font for longer content blocks.
- Use minimalist, line-based, and geometric icons that align with a luxury, modern, and uncluttered visual style.
- Emphasize large, impactful imagery and video content in full-width and masonry layouts, designed for mobile-first responsiveness, ensuring a stunning visual experience across all devices from 4K monitors to smartphones.
- Crucial element: Employ smooth scrolling, elegant entrance animations for components on scroll (fade and slide), seamless page transitions between routes, and satisfying micro-interactions for all interactive elements.
